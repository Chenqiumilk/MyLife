# Product Requirements: Diary Focus

## Goal

Build a calming diary and focus app that helps users record daily life, track mood, and connect focused work sessions back to a personal diary.

## Target Users

- Students and researchers who want gentle daily reflection.
- People who like cute visual journaling.
- Users who want lightweight focus tracking without a productivity-heavy interface.

## Core Loop

1. Open the calendar.
2. Pick a day.
3. Choose a mood.
4. Write diary notes, add photos, timestamps, and todos.
5. Start a focus session.
6. Complete focus.
7. Return to the diary and see completed focus time for the day.

## MVP Scope

- Calendar home page.
- Mood icons for past days.
- Diary editor with photos, timestamps, and todos.
- Focus timer with pet companion.
- Focus time summary in diary.
- Basic settings screen.

## Later Scope

- Book and movie review libraries.
- StoreKit purchase.
- Lock screen / Face ID.
- Notifications.
- iCloud backup.
- Multi-language support.

## Non-Goals for MVP

- Social sharing.
- Cloud sync.
- AI diary analysis.
- Complex analytics.
- Full App Store monetization.

## Success Criteria

- Users can create and reopen diary entries without data loss.
- Photos are stored reliably and duplicate uploads are deduplicated.
- Focus sessions correctly accumulate per day.
- The app feels calm, cute, and lightweight.
